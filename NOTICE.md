# Notices

## pi-intercom

This project's intercom tool surface (`send` / `ask` / `reply` / `list` / `pending` / `status`) and message protocol design are derived from [pi-intercom](https://github.com/nicobailon/pi-intercom) by Nico Bailon, used under the MIT License. pi-relay re-implements the design for cross-machine WebSocket transport rather than vendoring pi-intercom source.
